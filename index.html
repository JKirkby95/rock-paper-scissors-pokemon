PokéDuel is based off of the classic game rock, paper, scissors, lizard, spock.

  • If the user and the computer select the same Pokémon it's a draw.
  • Charmander will defeat Bulbasaur and Pidgey.
  • Squirtle will defeat Pikachu and Charmander.
  • Bulbasaur will defeat Squirtle and Pikachu.
  • Pikachu will defeat Charmander and Pidgey.
  • Pidgey will defeat Bulbasaur and Squirtle.
